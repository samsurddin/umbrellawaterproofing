Roofs. Siding. Decks

When it rains, water pours where it should with Umbrella Waterproofing.

The Simple Truth

If you have a non-slotted deck, balcony, plaza, flat roof, parking garage or mechanical room floor made of Concrete, Vinyl or Plywood Substrate, water or moisture exposure can cause mildew, stains, rot, cracks or collapse at or below the surface.

The Simple Solution

Pac-Poly ELASTO-DECK™ coating services from Umbrella Waterproofing.

Every five years*, Umbrella Waterproofing will restore the luster and integrity of your deck’s topcoat with a streak-free, low-fume, virtually odorless re-application of the highest quality sealants from Pacific Polymers (Pac-Poly). If you notice any signs of water damage (see list to the right), Umbrella will diagnose the source of the leak and repair your property before the waterproofing begins.
