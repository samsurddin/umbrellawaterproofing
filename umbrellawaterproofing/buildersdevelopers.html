Flat roofs, plazas and decks made of concrete, vinyl, non-slotted wood or other substrates should be professionally waterproofed to prevent leaks and structural corrosion.

Professional waterproofing and the application of deck sealant and roof sealant are not tasks within the capabilities of a general subcontractor. Failure to properly waterproof a new residential or commercial structure can lead to pricey internal leaks, deck failure and even structural failure down the road.
