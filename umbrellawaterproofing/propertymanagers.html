Property Managers

Take Control of Your Property

Properly draining your decks, roofs and walkways and garages with Umbrella Professional Waterproofing.

There are some calls you’re probably used to handling: leaky faucet, clogged toilet, locked door…Then there are tenant calls that should raise the caution flag: black spots forming on the ceiling, soft/damp drywall, a water drip that can’t be identified. These are common signs that water is leaking within your building. Leaky pipes aren’t always to blame — often the roof, deck, walkway or plaza needs to be repaired, resurfaced and resealed to ensure proper rainwater runoff.

One call is all it takes to save your clients from the surprise costs of easily preventable water-damage repairs. Umbrella Waterproofing can perform annual inspections in between regularly scheduled professional waterproofing services.

Flat roofs, plazas and decks made of concrete, vinyl, non-slotted wood or other substrates should be professionally waterproofed (about every five years) to prevent leaks and structural corrosion.

Residential Deck Repair

Umbrella Waterproofing and Umbrella Restoration are subsidiaries of Umbrella Construction, Inc. For more than a decade, Umbrella Restoration has been the company Western Washington turns to restore extensive water damage after it has already occurred. We work with builders, architects and insurance companies to diagnose problems and fully repair residential and commercial structures where waterproofing wasn’t properly maintained or installed in the first place.

Umbrella Waterproofing was founded to help homeowners, HOA’s, condo boards, property managers and builders to avoid these unnecessary repairs. We can analyze your property, exposure areas and drainage systems to identify all of the structural elements that will be protected by professional waterproofing.

Annual Inspections

If your property appears to be sound, let’s keep it that way. Annual Inspections from Umbrella Waterproofing go below the surface to detect potential problems before they arise. We will inspect your roof, decks, plazas, patios, parking, structural joints, siding and fascia, concrete planters, walkways and stairs, basements and foundation.

Along with your report, we will provide an honest assessment of how often you should schedule waterproofing treatment so that you can help your clients to plan accordingly.
